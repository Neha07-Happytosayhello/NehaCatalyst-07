About ReBorn Fashion+

At ReBorn Fashion+, we believe in giving clothes a second life. Our mission is to reduce textile waste by transforming pre-loved garments into unique, sustainable fashion pieces that tell a story of environmental consciousness and style.

Founded in 2020, we started as a small team of passionate environmentalists and fashion enthusiasts who saw the devastating impact of fast fashion on our planet. Every year, millions of tons of clothing end up in landfills, contributing to pollution and climate change. We decided to be part of the solution.

Our Mission

To revolutionize the fashion industry by creating a circular economy where every garment has multiple lives, reducing waste and promoting sustainable consumption patterns.

Our Vision

A world where fashion is circular, eco-conscious, and accessible to all. We envision a future where sustainable fashion is the norm, not the exception.

Our innovative process involves collecting pre-loved garments from individuals and businesses, carefully sorting and cleaning them, and then transforming them into unique pieces through creative upcycling techniques. Each item in our collection carries a story of sustainability and conscious consumption.

We partner with innovative designers and recycling experts to create collections that inspire. Our team is a passionate group of sustainability advocates, designers, and logistics experts working together to redefine the fashion industry.
